
# Generating Characters
Below is an outline of how to generate characters for ***Lambda***.

---

***Allocate your attributes.***

> Choose one of the following distributions.
> You allocate those six numbers among your hero's attributes.
> 2, 2, 2, 2, 0, 0
> 
> 4, 2, 1, 0, 0, 0
> 
> 5, 3, 1, 0, -1, -3
> 
> 6, 2, 0, -1, -1, -3

***Choose an archetype.***
> Familiarize yourself with your new abilities and skills.

***Calculate some numbers.***
> MOV = 30 + DEX * 2.5 (rounded towards 20 to the nearest 5)
> 
> HP = Base + CON
> 
> SP = 3 + (Max of INT, WIS) / 2
> 
> LOAD = 8 + STR

***Determine your Adventuring Gear.***
> Choose a peasant's weapon. Determine where you got such a weapon.
> 
> Gather one piece of adventuring equipment, at a standard quantity. 
> For instance, 30 ft. of rope, 10 rocks for use with a sling, three torches, or a tinderbox.
> 
> Determine your wealth. For a standard peasant it will be (1d4-1)*10 GP.
