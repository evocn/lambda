Adjustments for Session 4: Recurve bow -> Hunting bow

The Thief’s Stealth ability has been adjusted for Group Stealth. They can now guide up to two allies through a stealth scenario who are unimpeding.

Sneak Attack will be 2d6. However, you can attempt to regain the ability to use it by choosing a target, and trying to conceal yourself to them as an action on your turn. You make a stealth check against a DC variable by the scenario, but most likely against their perception, and if you succeed, you can make the sneak attack on the following turn. This is contingent on the scenario, though. You will be unsuccessful hiding against an opponent that has a clear line of sight.

Rage does not reduce incoming damage by half, but instead prevents the berserker from being reduced below 1 Health until the rage is over.
