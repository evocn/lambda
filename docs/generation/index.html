Generating Characters

Below is an outline of how to generate characters for Lambda.

Allocate your attributes.

Choose one of the following distributions. You allocate those six numbers among your hero’s attributes.

2, 2, 2, 2, 0, 0

4, 2, 1, 0, 0, 0

5, 3, 1, 0, -1, -3

6, 2, 0, -1, -1, -3

Choose an archetype.

Familiarize yourself with your new abilities and skills.

Select your equipment.

Choose a peasant’s weapon. Determine where you got such a weapon.

Pick one piece of adventuring equipment.

Determine your wealth. You have (1d4-1)*10 GP.

Finishing touches!

MOV = 30 + DEX * 2.5 (rounded towards 20 to the nearest 5)

HP = Base + CON

SP = 3 + (Max of INT, WIS) / 2

LOAD = 8 + STR

Give ’em a name!
